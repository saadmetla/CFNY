ABOUT

Capital Flight is a New York City-based record label, production company, and brand partner for the diaspora generation — the children of immigrants who grew up between two worlds and created a third. Our work lives in the culture before it reaches the mainstream.

The name says everything. In economics, capital flight is what happens when people and money leave a place in search of something better. It's the technical term for what millions of immigrant families did — packed up, crossed an ocean or a border, and built a new life in a city that didn't know their name yet. New York City is the result of that, repeated across a hundred different cultures. Every neighborhood is a different country. Every block has a different story. Capital Flight exists to tell those stories right.

Founded by Pakistani-American director and producer Saad Metla, our work spans the full range of that vision — from Migos to Diljit Dosanjh, from hip-hop to Bollywood, from New York to London. Saad's background in psychology shapes how we approach every project: the best content doesn't just look good, it makes people feel something they recognize but couldn't name before.

Through Capital Flight Records — distributed globally via Empire — we develop artists who exist between cultures and give them the infrastructure to build something real. Across production, marketing, and label services, we focus on one thing: movements that travel, not moments that fade.
